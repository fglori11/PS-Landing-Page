Meet the Partners

Felipe Gloria | Project Architect

Felipe Gloria, a proud Hispanic architect, is passionate about designing spaces that reflect the spirit of the communities they serve. With years of experience, he focuses on giving back to underserved communities. Growing up in the Chicagoland area, he's inspired by the strength and creativity of the Hispanic community. Through Pueblo Studio, Felipe aims to create functional, meaningful spaces that foster connection and growth, shaping neighborhoods that feel like home, celebrate culture, and bring people together. For him, architecture is about making a positive impact and contributing to projects that truly matter.

Geovanny Martinez | Project Manager

Geovanny Martinez, a proud Hispanic professional with extensive project management experience, excels in overseeing projects with strong collaboration, organization, and attention to detail. Growing up in Mexico, he's inspired by the resilience of the Mexican community and focuses on projects that positively impact underserved areas. For Geovanny, project management goes beyond deadlines—it's about creating empowering spaces that honor culture and community needs. He is dedicated to contributing to projects that strengthen and enrich communities.
